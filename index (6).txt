Marketing Partnership — Not a Donation
BAC offers marketing services invoices — never donation receipts — for corporate outreach. Sponsors receive map placement, flyer distribution, and co-branding with the Third Friday Art Walk. Packages: $100 Business Membership · $150 Map Spot · $275 Map Bundle (2 spots) · $200 Flyer · $2,500 Corporate · $3,000+ Large Corporate. Director: Takako Konishi · Marketing: Claireasa Golden · Photographer: Emily Robinson
